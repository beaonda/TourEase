PICO DE LORO Nasugbu Batangas
Pico de Loro is a haven of eco-friendly tourism tucked behind the mountains and a lush cover of trees as far as the eyes can see on one side and the blue expanse of the West Philippine Sea on the other. It is primarily for members but they do have Pico Sand Hotel for walk-in guests but do book way in advance because in the Philippines, it is always summer. The design and architecture is very modern and takes great advantage of the breeze blowing from the ocean. There are golf carts to take you around the resort and very courteous staff that attend to your every need. Pico de Loro offers a host of indoor and outdoor activities that will surely make your stay the most memorable of all.

MOUNT BATULAO Nasugbu Batangas
Want to get away from the city? Mt. Batulao is regarded as one of the best destinations for trekking and mountain climbing because of its accessibility to the metropolis, particularly for beginners. You'll be able to take in the fresh breeze, stunning rock formations, and vast meadows in two to three hours. The stunning 360-degree panorama of Batangas and its adjacent cities, as well as the gorilla-shaped ridge line near the summit, are also must-sees. The ascent is not particularly strenuous. Because the residents are so welcoming and friendly, safety is also not a concern. You only need to carry a spirit of pleasure and adventure on your journey up to Mt. Batulao.
